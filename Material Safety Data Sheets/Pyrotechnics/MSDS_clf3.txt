[head=3]Section 2. Hazard(s) & First Aid[/head]

[bold]Potential Acute Health Effects:[/bold]
Severe burns, Chemical Poisoning, Caustic Effects, Death

[bold]Potential Chronic Health Effects:[/bold]
Severe burns, Chemical Poisoning, Caustic Effects, Death

[bold]Incase of ingestion,[/bold]
Induce vomitting or inject activated charcoal immediately, apply ointment to burns and take an anti-toxin agent such as Dylovene. Seek medical attention for caustic damages.

[bold]Incase of injection,[/bold]
Seek urgent medical attention immediately. Ensure ointment is applied to burns as quickly as possible, use an anti-toxin agent such as dylovene as well as a burn medication such as dermaline.

[bold]Flammability Considerations:[/bold]
\[  ] Highly Flammable
\[  ] Store in a sealed container
\[X] May Spontaneously Combust
