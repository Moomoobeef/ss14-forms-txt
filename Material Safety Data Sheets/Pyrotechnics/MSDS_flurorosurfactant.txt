[head=3]Section 2. Hazard(s) & First Aid[/head]

This chemical will create a chemical foam that can expand to 500 times its original volume when mixed with water. This foam can also carry other chemicals that were in solution with the flurorosurfactant into the foam, creating a wide area containing said chemicals.

This can lead to accidental ingestion of solution chemicals. For this reason flurorosurfactant is to be treated with the utmost care and kept in a sealed container away from water.
